ABOUT

Frawlsalla is a 2D platform fighting based game. Updates and patches will be continuously released for each 2 months, Frawlsalla will also hold tournaments both for public and esport category. Players can participate in both normal and ranked match, so that they can experience the game based on their skills. Frawlsalla is 100% Free to Play on PS5, PS4, Xbox Series X, Xbox One, PC, Nintendo Switch, and Mobile Devices.
